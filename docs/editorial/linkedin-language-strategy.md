## Role of LinkedIn

LinkedIn is used only for:

- author identity;
- publisher identity;
- content distribution;
- external profile verification.

LinkedIn is **not** a factual source for:

- casino availability;
- payment methods;
- bonuses or wagering;
- licences;
- KYC or regulatory claims.

Do not scrape LinkedIn for images, job history, education, followers, endorsements, certifications, or posts to populate the site.

Do not invent external publication URLs.

Do not invent experience or credentials.

## Personal LinkedIn — Arturs Stoliks

- **English-first.**
- Prefer short English summaries of selected JugadaMax research.
- May include personal editorial observations.
- Link to English/global JugadaMax content when available.
- When only a Spanish article exists, the post may summarize it in English and clearly indicate that the linked article is in Spanish.

## Company LinkedIn — JugadaMax

- **Spanish-first.**
- Primary audience: Mexico and LATAM.
- Spanish posts for Spanish guides, reviews, bonus changes, operator updates, and news.
- English company posts only for genuine `/en` content, global operator coverage, or international company communication.
- Do **not** mix full Spanish and English duplicates inside one long post.
- Use separate posts when both languages are genuinely needed.

## Post format

- One main language per post.
- Prefer a concise summary plus a link rather than full article duplication.
- Disclose affiliation when commercially relevant, consistent with site disclosure norms.
- Never invent verification badges, awards, or “expert” claims not already published honestly on jugadamax.com.
